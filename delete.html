Request Data Deletion – TurdTracker Pro

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your personal data collected by TurdTracker Pro, developed by Gut Feeling Labs.

What data do we store?

  • Nickname / display name
  • Bowel movement logs and Bristol Stool Scale entries
  • Health scores and trend data
  • Device identifiers (via Firebase Analytics)

What data will be deleted?

Upon request, we will permanently delete all personal data associated with your account, including your nickname, all health tracking entries, and any analytics data linked to your device identifier.

Data retention note

After receiving your deletion request, we will process and confirm deletion within 30 days. Some anonymized, aggregated data (not linked to you personally) may be retained for statistical purposes.

1
Send an email to Sai5412016@gmail.com
2
Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request – TurdTracker Pro"
3
Include your nickname or any information that helps us identify your data
4
We will confirm deletion within 30 days
