Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gut Feeling Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application TurdTracker Pro (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address when you register an account.
  • Health & wellness data: Bowel movement logs, Bristol Stool Scale entries, health scores, and related wellness tracking data you enter into the App.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers, collected automatically for analytics and crash reporting purposes.
  • Usage data: Information about how you use the App, including features accessed and time spent, collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Camera data: If you use the photo/measurement feature, the App accesses your camera. Images are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App and its features
  • To personalize your experience and calculate health scores

  • To display relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • To process in-app purchases and subscriptions (via Google Play)
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Analytics – Firebase

Firebase Analytics data is processed by Google LLC.
